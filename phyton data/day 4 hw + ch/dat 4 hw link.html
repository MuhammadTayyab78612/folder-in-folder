1. I am writing to extend a cordial invitation to your institution to participate in the Sadiq Entrepreneurial Summit, hosted by Sadiq Public School.

  2. As the Principal and CEO of Sadiq Public School, it is my pleasure to share with you a brief history of our esteemed institution before detailing the significance of this event.

  3. Established in 1953 by His Highness Sir Sadiq Mohammad Khan Abbasi, the Ameer of Bahawalpur, Sadiq Public School holds a proud legacy of academic excellence and holistic education.

  4. The foundation stone of our school was laid on 4th March 1953, with His Highness graciously providing 450 acres of land and financing the entire construction.

  5. Since then, Sadiq Public School has been dedicated to nurturing young minds, instilling values of leadership, innovation and service.

  6. It is with great enthusiasm that we announce the Sadiq Entrepreneurial Summit, scheduled to take place from 9th August to 11th August 2024.

  7. This landmark event marks the first-ever entrepreneurial summit in southern Punjab organised by a school and reflects our commitment to fostering an entrepreneurial spirit among the youth of our region.

  8. The Sadiq Entrepreneurial Summit aims to bring together students, educators, entrepreneurs and industry leaders to explore the dynamic world of entrepreneurship.

  9. Through engaging keynote sessions, interactive workshops and insightful panel discussions attendees will have the opportunity to gain valuable knowledge, network with like-minded individuals and unlock their potential as future leaders and innovators.

  10. We believe that by providing a platform for collaboration and learning, we can empower the next generation of entrepreneurs to drive positive change and contribute to the socio-economic development of our communities.

  11. We would be honoured to have your institution join us at the Sadiq Entrepreneurial Summit.

  12. Your participation and support will play a crucial role in making this event a resounding success.

  13. Please mark your calendars for 9th August to 11th August 2024 and stay tuned for further details regarding the agenda, speakers, and the registration process which will be shared with you in the coming weeks.

  14. Thank you for considering our invitation. We look forward to welcoming you to Sadiq Public School for this momentous occasion.

  15. Yours faithfully,
